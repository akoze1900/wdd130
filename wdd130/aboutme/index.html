About Me

Paul Amienoho Oghayerio

Hello everybody! I am Paul Amienoho Oghayerio. I am from Benin City, Edo State, Nigeria, West Africa. I was born on 22nd October, 1957.

I am married to Sister Clara Oghayerio. We were sealed at the Aba, Nigeria Temple. I am blessed with seven children and fifteen grand children.

I join the Church in 2012. Presently, I am the Branch President of Queen Ede Branch in the Oregbeni Nigerria Stake.

At my spare time, I listen to country music and the Hymns of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints.
